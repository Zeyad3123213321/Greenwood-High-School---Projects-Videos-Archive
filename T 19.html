About Our School

Founded in 1985, Greenwood High School has been a cornerstone of educational excellence in our community for nearly four decades. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment that fosters intellectual growth, creativity, and character development.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for the challenges of higher education and beyond. Our diverse student body represents the rich cultural tapestry of our community.
